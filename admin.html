Roles & What They Can Do

RolePermissions
EmployeeSubmit certificate requests; view and download only their own certificates
HR StaffApprove or reject non-salary certificate requests; view all employees' requests (read-only)
HR DirectorApprove or reject salary certificate requests, entering the official salary figure at approval time; view all employees' requests (read-only)
System AdministratorUser and role management only — create/edit/delete users, assign roles, enable/disable accounts, review signup requests. No access to certificate requests or approvals.
